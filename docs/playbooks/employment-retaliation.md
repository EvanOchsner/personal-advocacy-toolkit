# Playbook: employment retaliation

*(Stub — authored in Phase 5.)*

> **Reference material, not legal advice.**

Covers: EEOC and state fair-employment agency channels, short statutes
of limitations (often 180/300 days for Title VII), OSHA whistleblower
channels, state labor-commission wage-and-hour paths, NLRB for concerted
activity, and when to engage a plaintiff-side employment attorney.
