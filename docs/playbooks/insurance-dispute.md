# Playbook: insurance dispute

*(Stub — authored in Phase 5 with MD as the worked jurisdiction.)*

> **This is reference material, not legal advice.** It describes mechanics
> and points at authorities. It does not tell you what to argue in your
> specific case.

Covers: common patterns in first-party claim handling (auto total loss,
property, health), where unfair-claims-practice statutes typically live,
what a state Department of Insurance complaint pipeline usually looks
like, when a public adjuster or counsel starts to matter, how the
appraisal clause typically works, and when the question crosses from
claim-handling into bad-faith litigation.
