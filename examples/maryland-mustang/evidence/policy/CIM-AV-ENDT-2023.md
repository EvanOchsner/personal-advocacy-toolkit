## Section 1 — Schedule of Value

1.1 The *Agreed Value* for the insured vehicle is the amount stated
on the Declarations Page, established at policy inception by mutual
agreement between insured and insurer.

## Section 2 — Payment Upon Total Loss

2.1 (a) Upon total loss, the insurer shall pay the Agreed Value, less
any applicable deductible, as the measure of indemnity. The insurer
shall not substitute an independent or vendor-derived Actual Cash
Value for the Agreed Value, except where:

    (i)  the insurer establishes by a preponderance that the insured
         procured the policy by fraud or material misrepresentation
         with respect to the vehicle's identity, condition, or value;
         or

    (ii) the insured, in writing, requests reopening of the Schedule
         of Value at renewal.

2.2 (b) A valuation commissioned by the insurer or by an appraisal
vendor retained by the insurer does not displace the Agreed Value.
It may inform repair-versus-total-loss analysis, but it is not a
substitute measure of indemnity.

## Section 3 — Partial Loss

3.1 For partial loss, the insurer pays the cost of repair at
customary rates as defined in CIM-VEH-2023 Article VII, not to
exceed the Agreed Value.
