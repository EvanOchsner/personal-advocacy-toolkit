Personal Advocacy Toolkit

Do the legwork so whoever helps you can actually help you.

An AI toolkit for non-technical people in a fact-heavy dispute — bad-faith insurance claims, surprise medical bills, harassment campaigns, retaliating landlords, debt collectors who won't follow the rules.

PAT does the legwork: evidence intake with forensic integrity, situation-specific reference material, drafting tools with anti-hallucination guard rails, and a publication-safety pipeline. The output is something an attorney, regulator, or journalist can act on.
